08th June 2019

Social Media and You

We're so connected through fb, insta and twitter
But we're still alone and this truth is real bitter
Whole day chatting on WhatsApp, Messenger and hike
We almost forgot what meeting is really like
We have to choose things according to the trend
Now be it clothes, accessories, jewel or friend
Today being lazy, entertaining and funny is coolish
And being hardworking and determined is foolish
You have to be like everybody to be accepted as their clique
But how can you forget you are you and you're unique
